Spartan Parker

Spartan Parker is an I.O.T solution to the horrendous parking situation in urban downtowns throughout the United States, where commuters have no information in advance whether an parking structure is full.

The objective of Spartan Parker is to notify the commuter that if the parking structure near them is full and if so redirect them to an parking lot that has openings. For the parking lot management Spartan Parker will automate parking lot management by validating cars in parking spaces, and giving real-time critical data to management.

  • Develop an dashboard for parking lot authority that gives clear and concise information where an specific car is parked.
